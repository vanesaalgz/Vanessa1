My Portfolio

Project 1

"This pencil drawing portrays an adorable puppy with remarkable detail. The artist has skillfully captured the softness of the puppy’s fur through delicate shading and fine lines, bringing the texture to life. The puppy’s bright, curious eyes are full of warmth and innocence, instantly drawing the viewer in. Its small, floppy ears and slightly tilted head give the drawing a playful and endearing quality. The attention to detail in the paws and gentle curves of the body further emphasize the tenderness and charm of this young pup."

Project 2

"This pencil drawing beautifully captures the serene elegance of a young girl. With soft, detailed strokes, the artist has highlighted the delicacy of her facial features, such as her large, expressive eyes that seem to tell a story of their own. Her meticulously shaded hair falls in gentle waves, framing her face and adding a touch of grace. The subtle shading and intricate details in her skin and clothing reflect a refined technique, making this portrait a work that conveys both calmness and emotional depth."
